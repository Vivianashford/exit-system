The Problem

6 million service business owners will reach retirement age by 2035. Only 20% of listed businesses actually sell. The ones that do often sell for far less than they should.

The #1 reason: owner dependency. When the business can't run without the founder, buyers see risk - and risk compresses multiples. The gap between a 3x exit and a 6x exit is transferability.
