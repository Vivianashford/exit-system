Dual Completion SWD Well

Simultaneous Injection & Oil Recovery Schematic

Injection String (RED)
Frac pump pushes waste DOWN through dedicated tubing, through the packer, into disposal formation perforations.
Production String (GREEN)
ESP or rod pump pulls accumulated oil UP through second tubing string to surface separator & storage.
Packer — Zone Isolation
Mechanical seal between production zone (oil above) and injection zone (disposal below). This is the key piece.
Casing & Wellhead
Existing production casing and dual-completion wellhead. Surface casing cemented to protect fresh water per RRC Rule 13.
RRC Permit Requirements

Statewide Rules 9 & 46 require injected fluids remain confined to the authorized interval.

Dual completion must be indicated on Form W-2/G-1 completion report.

Recommended strategy: Apply to expand the "authorized injection interval" to include both the oil zone and disposal zone — this simplifies MIT requirements and regulatory oversight.

Mechanical Integrity Test (MIT) required. Max pressure: ½ psi per foot to top of injection interval.
